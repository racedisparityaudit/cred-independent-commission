Government response to the Commission on Race and Ethnic Disparities

In its response to the Commission’s report, the government sets out its inclusion strategy and an action plan for building a fairer and more inclusive society in the long term.

The government response includes specific recommendations and accountability measures. It provides a template for the rest of the public sector – as well as businesses, charities, or individuals – to work towards achieving a society which is truly equal.
